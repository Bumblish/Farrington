Membership

The Fruit Fanatic group is a lively online community that celebrates the diverse world of fruits, from the zesty citrus family to the sweet wonders of berries, tropical delights, and more. Within this vibrant group, known as the Fruit Fusion Fellowship, enthusiasts come together to share their love for a medley of fruits, connecting over the shared joy of flavors and the nutritional goodness each fruit brings. Whether you're captivated by the refreshing burst of citrus or enchanted by the sweetness of berries and tropical treats, the Fruit Fusion Fellowship invites you to join this virtual space. Here, members exchange recipes, cultivation tips, and explore the rich tapestry of fruit varieties. Embark on a fruity journey where friendship and flavor come together in a delightful mix!

About

Started in 1954 by our founder, Sir Fruitington III, the Fruit Fanatic was created to be a safe haven for all those seeking fruit related compadres. After being raided by the IRS in 2007 for tax evasion, the organization was forced to move its operations into the digital realm. Dispite the setbacks, the Fruit Fanatics are still the largest fruit loving conglmerate operating in the United States. With activity in all 50 states and several sitting members of the United States Congress, we will continue to fight for fruit and fruit lovers everywhere. See our membership page for information about the Fruit Fanatics.
